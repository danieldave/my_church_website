Welcome

You are warmly welcome to Methodist Church Ijoku, Sagamu Circuit. We are a circuit of the Methodist Church Nigeria, committed to the teachings of Jesus Christ and the Methodist tradition of faith, discipline, and service.

We invite you to worship with us and grow together in faith, love, and Christian service.

Our Vision

To spread Scriptural holiness throughout the land.

Our Mission

To promote the Christian faith and practice through worship, evangelism, discipleship, education, and social responsibility.
