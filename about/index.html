About Our Church

Rooted in Methodist heritage, committed to Christian service, and guided by the vision of the Methodist Church Nigeria.

Our Heritage

Methodist Church Ijoku

Methodist Church Ijoku is a congregation under the Sagamu Circuit of the Methodist Church Nigeria. The church stands as a testimony of faith, sacrifice, and commitment to Christian growth within the Ijoku community.

As part of the global Methodist tradition, we uphold the teachings of John Wesley, emphasizing holiness of heart and life, evangelism, and service to humanity.

Our Calling

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To raise a God-fearing people who will be equipped spiritually and morally to impact the church and society positively.

Our Mission

To spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ through worship, teaching, evangelism, and social responsibility in line with the mission of the Methodist Church Nigeria.

Leadership

Church & Circuit Oversight

The Sagamu Circuit is overseen by a Presbyter appointed by the Methodist Church Nigeria. The Presbyter works with stewards, society leaders, and various church groups to ensure spiritual growth and administrative order.
